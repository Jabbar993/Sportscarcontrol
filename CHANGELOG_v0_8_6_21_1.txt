SCC v0.8.6.21.1 · ACO Importer v2.1

- ACO Importer v2.1 now separates inputs into:
  - Race Classification PDF (single file)
  - Fastest Laps PDF (single file, optional fallback from results)
  - Qualifying / Hyperpole PDFs (1..N files)
- Multiple qualifying/hyperpole PDFs are parsed and merged into performance.poles.
- Qualifying class is auto-detected from filename/content where possible: HYP, LMGT3, LMP2, LMP2 P/A, LMP3, GTE Am.
- Preview now shows a readiness checklist and the file groups used for Results, FL and PP.
- Race Control remains manual.
- app.js and existing race data were not changed.
